About Me

Hello! I'm Ceren Kepenek. I was born on November 2, 2002, in the beautiful lands of Kocaeli, Turkey. I completed my education from elementary school to high school in Kocaeli. Currently, I am a senior student in the Department of Information Systems and Technologies at Istanbul Okan University, and I am also pursuing a double major in Software Engineering.

As of now, I am taking part in the Erasmus program in Poland at Opole University of Technology. This experience adds significant richness to both my academic and cultural perspectives.

While creating my website, I aim to share information about my projects and content. Throughout this process, I highlight my curiosity in the world of software and my passion for learning, continuing to explore the power of technology.

As a personal note, I have a cute bird named "Lemon." I love animals, and my favorite color is blue. I continue to enhance my skills in this field by blending my colorful world with technology.

On my website, I will share this exciting journey and my projects. I hope you have the opportunity to get to know me better on this platform. Thank you!
